|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|

| T-1-12 | Tampering | Negative or non-numeric duration injected | mitigate | PositiveIntegerField + serializer validation (Task 1) |
| T-1-13 | Spoofing | Bulk-update without CSRF | mitigate | Inherits CsrfViewMiddleware + X-CSRFToken from Plan 01 client |
| T-1-14 | Denial of Service | Bulk-update with huge payload | accept | Admin-only authenticated endpoint, low-value target, internal academic tool; DATA_UPLOAD_MAX_MEMORY_SIZE caps request size |

All threats dispositioned. No HIGH-severity unmitigated threat.
